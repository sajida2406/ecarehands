MODULES

Core Modules

The ecarehand Total Hospital management software is a comprehensive solution that offers end to end solution, effectively addresses all the challenges in day today operations and allows healthcare service providers to focus on patient care.


Inbuilt Dashboards

  • Patient registration process.
  • For various Types of dashboard to track, monitor Key Performance Indicator, metrics.

Front Desk

  • Patient registrations.
  • Queue Management interface.
  • Various Types of enquiries.
  • Patient Feedback / Complaint Management.

Outpatient Department (OPD)

  • Track patients’ appointments across different OPDs of various doctors at different locations within hospital and outside.

Inpatient Department (IPD)

  • 360 degree view of patient information.
  • Manage Admits on various wards span across different floors of the various building(s).
  • Admissions, Discharge & Transfers.

Nursing Station

  • Manage Treatment charts of patients with respect to doctor daily notes.
  • Monitor health of patient on various parameters.
  • Ordering of various items to support patient's health tracking.

Operation Theatre

  • Record procedure information.
  • Preoperative checklist.
  • Operation theatre bookings for various OTs.
  • Manage surgical inventory.


Laboratory Tests

  • Path Lab test ordering.
  • Configure lab Test(s) and parameters as per changing needs and research findings.
  • Sample infromation and processing details.

Laundry Module

  • Manage different Laundry items and service vendors
  • Demand, Delivery and Billing information.
  • Lost and found with damage tracking.

Blood Bank

  • Health check, Blood collection, Screening, separation, manage stock
  • Requisitions, issue of blood and billing.
  • Disposals.

General Ledger management

  • General Ledger and Profit & loss accounts
  • budgeting forecasting of departmental cost centres. Payables, Receivables, Assets, Cash book, various Reciliations.

Document Management

  • Documents keeping at patient level to retrive easily for reference
  • Manage differnt folder for photos, insurance papers, x-rays, issued letters, etc.
  • Administrators can set different predefined letter formats for different requirements.

Store Management (Inventory)

  • Capture item Indents that needs to be ordered.
  • Demand, Quotation, Award with Purchase Order, GRN.
  • Invoice, Payment voucher and release payment.
  • Manage stock at central and at various locations.
  • Issue stock to differnt locations.

Communications

  • Single/bulk SMS
  • emaild Notifications.
  • Whatsapp messages.
Management Infromation system
  • Various drill down reports to support dashboards

Pharmacy Management

  • Medicine durg specifications
  • Medicine stock, issue and bill.

Electronic Medical Record (EMR)

  • Patient Demographic.
  • Treatment history on various configurable monitoring parameters.
  • Family history.
  • Immunisation Details for the refernce point.
  • Information of Implant devices, Prescriptions, advice laboratory tests.
  • Life style infromation.

Billing & Insurance
